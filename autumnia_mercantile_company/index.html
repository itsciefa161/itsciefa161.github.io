About the Autumnia Mercantile Company

The Autumnia Mercantile Company is a neutral company focusing on trade. The main goal is to generate wealth for every member through exploitation of the resources available in Adalian.



Core Principles:


Neutrality and Ethical Trading:

Inspired by his past, Ciefa places righteousness at the core of his operations.


Community First:

The company will use its influence to better the conditions for all Members, prioritizing collective well-being over profit.


Discipline and Efficiency:

Reflecting his own journey, the organizational culture emphasizes hard work, reliability, and respect for all.


Democratic Governance:

The DAO Council is aimed at fostering a collective, empowering decision-making process that honors the dignity of each member.


Divisions:

DAO Council - The Syndicate:

Comprising the Chiefs of each division and the Founder, the council offers an internally transparent, inclusive governance model.



Autumnia Fiscal Guild (AFG) - Finance Division:

Responsible for managing the treasury, budget allocation, and financial risk assessment.



Autumnia Diplomatic Corps (ADC) - Diplomatic Division:

To negotiate trade agreements and foster inter-entity relationships.



Autumnia Ore Ventures (AOV) - Mining Division:

Specializing in asteroid mining and resource allocation.



Autumnia Industrial Solutions (AIS) - Industry Division:

Focused on refining, manufacturing, and infrastructure provision.



Autumnia Shield (AS) - Defensive Military Division:

Tasked with asset and personnel protection, crisis, and emergency responses.



Autumnia Strike Force (ASF) - Offensive Military Division:

Developed for strategic offensive capabilities.


In the Autumnia Mercantile Company, every member is essential to the success of the company.

About the Founder, Ciefa

The story of Ciefa is one of resilience and triumph over adversity. Born into servitude, he worked in the harsh conditions of a mining company as a slave. His life was one of toil and struggle, yet Ciefa always harbored dreams of something greater. Through sheer determination and a series of serendipitous events, he gained his freedom. Through odd jobs and leveraging his innate skills, Ciefa scraped together enough funds to purchase not just his freedom but a future—a modest asteroid, a rudimentary ship, and a loyal crew. Armed with a spirit forged in the crucible of hardship, he resolved to create an organization where ethics and dignity would be pillars, not afterthoughts.

DAO Overview

Each member of the Autumnia Mercantile Company will be a member of the DAO.

Important decisions about the strategy and treasury will be held through the DAO.

The weight for the votes will depend on the role of the voter. For example, a vote about usage of funds will give more weight to votes from someone in the Treasury division

The DAO will initially consists out of the Founder and the 6 Heads of each division.

Treasury, Investments and Taxes

The goal of the Autumnia Mercantile Company is to generate wealth for everyone.


If members generate income through the alliance means, they're subject to paying 5% taxes.

The funds will be allocated as follows:


• 30% to New Member Fund: We support new members and help them to get started.
• 25% to Company Treasury: These funds will be used for investments the DAO has voted for.
• 35% to Industry Treasury: To keep expanding, we'll use funds from the Industry Treasury.
• 10% to Military Treasury: We are neutral by standard. But we will not give up without a fight.


The funds will have to be sent on a bi-weekly basis by each member through a Smart Contract that sends the funds automatically to the Company's Treasury which is guarded by the Council through a MultiSig Safe.
